## SECTION 2: IMPACT BENEFIT AGREEMENTS (IBAs) - THE CONTRACTUAL LEVERAGE

### 2.1 What is an Impact Benefit Agreement?

**Legal Definition**:
An IBA is a binding contract between:

- Mining company (private entity)
- Government authorities (national and local)
- Often includes indigenous communities as signatory parties

**Scope of Obligations**:

- Environmental management and remediation
- Community benefit provisions
- Employment and training requirements
- Infrastructure development
- Revenue sharing
- Dispute resolution mechanisms
- Modification/termination clauses

**Enforceability**:

- IBAs are binding contracts under international law
- Disputes trigger contract law, not just regulatory frameworks
- Can be litigated in domestic courts and international arbitration
- Breach creates liability and damages claims

### 2.2 Amaroq’s Nalunaq IBA (APPROVED JANUARY 8, 2026)

1. Naalakkersuisut (Greenlandic Home Rule Government)
